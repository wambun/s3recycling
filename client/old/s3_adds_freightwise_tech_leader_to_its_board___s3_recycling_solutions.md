# S3 Adds Tech and Logistics Leader To Its Board

**SPRINGFIELD**, Tenn. – S3 Recycling Solutions today announced that it has named Chris Cochran to its eight-member Board. Cochran is the CEO of [FreightWise](https://www.freightwisellc.com/), a Nashville-based technology growth company celebrated for its innovations in the logistics tech sector.

Cochran is expected to contribute extensive expertise in logistics technology, operational efficiency, mergers and acquisitions, and transformative leadership. As CEO of FreightWise, he has helped the organization garner numerous accolades, including being recognized as America’s 2nd fastest-growing private company. Cochran was also named a finalist for Ernst and Young’s 2023 Entrepreneur of the Year award and recently recognized as a Power 100 executive by the Nashville Business Journal.

![Chris Cochran](https://s3rs.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/Chris-Cochran-S3-2024.jpg)

Chris Cochran, CEO of FreightWise

“Chris Cochran brings invaluable expertise to our board at S3 Recycling Solutions,” said Brian Moyer, S3 Board Chairman. “As a highly accomplished entrepreneur, his proven track record of leading a fast-growing company and his deep knowledge of the logistics industry will be instrumental in driving our vision forward. We are thrilled to welcome Chris to our board and look forward to his insightful contributions as we continue to innovate and expand.”

S3’s leadership also expressed the importance of adding a new board member who can help support S3’s recent geographical expansion and revenue growth.

“Chris’ expertise will be a significant asset as S3 continues to scale its business operations throughout North America,” said Rod McDaniel, S3 CEO. “Our recent [acquisition](https://s3rs.com/2024/07/acquisition/) in California and our new regional presence on the West Coast will be greatly enhanced by the supply chain logistics and operations experience that Chris has.”

Cochran has also served as a mentor for startups, a judge at entrepreneurial pitch events, and a board member for various organizations, including serving as finance chair of the Nashville Technology Council.

“Chris’ commitment to leadership and uplifting communities aligns seamlessly with S3’s mission to foster sustainability and innovation in recycling solutions,” McDaniel continued.

In addition to his executive achievements, Cochran successfully led the acquisition of Kuebix from Trimble (NASDAQ: TRMB), further demonstrating his expertise in corporate growth and strategic leadership. Prior to FreightWise, he held leadership roles at TracBack, Oracle, and Kronos, where he honed his skills in enterprise software, operations, and team development.

“I am honored to join S3 Recycling Solutions’ Board,” Cochran said. “I look forward to supporting Rod McDaniel and his groundbreaking efforts in advancing the recycling industry.”

**About S3** – Stewardship. Security. Sustainability. S3 Recycling Solutions is a full-service technology recycling firm with an emphasis on cost-effective processes for receiving, sorting, re-purposing and re-marketing electronic assets. We help our customers reduce the cost of ownership of their electronic technology assets, while providing a peace of mind that data security is maintained at the highest industry standards. Since its inception in 2007, S3 has supported a diverse client base throughout the United States and Canada. S3’s vision is to combat our planet’s e-waste epidemic by employing both environmentally and financially responsible processes for a stronger, safer global community.
