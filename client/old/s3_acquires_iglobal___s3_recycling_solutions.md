## S3 Recycling Solutions Acquires iGlobal Asset Management

We are thrilled to announce that S3 has acquired iGlobal Asset Management, a California-based leader in the ITAD and medical asset disposition industry. The acquisition marks a significant milestone in S3’s overall mission.

![S3 iGlobal](https://s3rs.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/S3-iGlobal-web.jpg)

**Enhanced Services**

With the integration of iGlobal Asset Management’s expertise and resources, we are expanding our service offerings to include specialized medical asset disposition services. Our combined teams bring a wealth of experience, ensuring we continue to meet and exceed industry standards.

**Commitment to Sustainability**

Together, we are more committed than ever to promoting sustainable asset disposition practices for both IT and medical assets. Our enhanced capabilities will help clients reduce their environmental footprint while maximizing the value of their retired assets.

**Client Benefits**

Access to a broader range of services and solutions tailored to meet diverse IT and medical asset disposition needs. Continued focus on data security, compliance, and environmental responsibility. Improved efficiency and innovation in our processes and technologies.

**S3 Certifications and Global Reach**

S3 is certified to R2, ISO 9001, 14001, and 45001, ensuring the highest standards in data security and environmental management. While iGlobal has served clients regionally, S3’s global reach allows us to support clients worldwide.

**Expansion and Facilities**

This acquisition marks the addition of S3’s third facility, bringing our combined square footage to 200,000 sqft. S3 has been in business since 2007, demonstrating our long-term commitment and stability in the industry.

**Recognition and Growth**

S3 has been named one of the best places to work in 2022 and 2024, highlighting our dedication to creating a positive work environment. We have made the Inc. 5000 fastest-growing companies list for the last four consecutive years, reflecting our continuous growth and innovation. With over 300 clients across North America, S3 continues to expand its reach and impact.

**Dedicated Client Service**

S3 has a dedicated client service team, ensuring that all our clients receive personalized and attentive support.

**Future Vision**

This acquisition is just the beginning of our growth journey. We are excited about the future and our ability to serve our clients with even greater dedication and expertise. Stay tuned for updates as we continue to innovate and expand our offerings.
