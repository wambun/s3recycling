# Information Technology Asset Disposition

Full Life-Cycle Management

### Security. Sustainability. Stewardship.

Information technology asset disposition is a process that involves receiving, sorting, testing, data destruction, remarketing and end-of-life recycling. S3 is a national leader in the ITAD industry, serving clients in more than 30 states across the U.S. and can provide client-specific e-waste solutions with assurances of security and sustainability throughout the process.

### Data Wiping Standards

S3 guarantees the destruction of clients’ data and meets the following ITAD data wiping standards:

- Air Force System Security 5020
- Department of Defense – 5220.28-STD
- Department of Defense – 5220-22-M
- Navy Staff Office
- NIST 800-88r1 Clear
- Gutmann All
- Gutmann Rll 1,7
- Gutmann Rll 2,7

- Bruce Schneier’s Algorithm
- Pfitzner Algorithm 33-Pass
- Pfitzner Algorithm 7-Pass
- AR 380-19
- British HMG InfoSec Standard 5
- Canada RCMP TSSIT OPS-II
- German VSITR
- Russian GOST-R 50739-95
