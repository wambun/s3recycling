## E-Recycling Made Easy

S3 Recycling Solutions is the right choice for businesses in need of an easy, hassle-free way to responsibly manage excess electronics. S3’s Springfield, Tenn. facility is R2-certified, and we offer on-site pickups across the country. We also provide customers with the peace of mind that data security is maintained at the highest industry standards. The S3 team handles the entire process of sorting, selling, recycling and/or donating surplus items based on clients’ needs, as well as any special requests. [Contact S3](https://s3rs.com/contact-us/) today to get started on your e-waste solution.
