## Who We Are

Headquartered in Springfield, Tenn., S3 Recycling Solutions’ vision is to combat our planet’s e-waste epidemic by employing both environmentally and financially responsible processes for a stronger, safer global community. We help our customers reduce the cost of ownership of their electronic technology assets, while providing a peace of mind that data security is maintained at the highest industry standards.

Organizations throughout North America rely on S3 to responsibly dispose of their excess technology devices. We create client-specific e-waste solutions that dispose of surplus equipment in a safe, secure and environmentally responsible manner. We accommodate for data and document security destruction needs through our rigorous security protocol at our facility, which maintains the highest industry standards.

S3 Recycling Solutions is a full-service technology recycling firm with an emphasis on cost-effective processes for receiving, sorting, repurposing and remarketing electronic assets. Our headquarters is in Springfield, Tenn., where our R2-certified facility is located. We help our customers reduce the cost of ownership of their electronic technology assets, while providing a peace of mind that data security is maintained at the highest industry standards. Since its inception in 2007, S3 has supported a diverse client base throughout the United States and Canada. S3’s vision is to combat our planet’s e-waste epidemic by employing both environmentally and financially responsible processes for a stronger, safer global community.

## R2

R2 certification, the electronic recycling industry’s top credential, ensures proper processes, safety measures and documentation are followed to responsibly recycle tech assets.

## ISO 14001

ISO 14001 certification: for organizations seeking to systematically manage its responsibilities in a way that contributes to the environmental pillar of sustainability.

## PJR UKAS ANAB

Perry Johnson Registrars audited and certified S3’s environmental management system regarding data destruction and the handling of used electronic devices.

## ISO 45001

ISO 45001 certification: specifies requirements for an occupational health and safety management system, to enable organizations to provide safe and healthy workplaces.
